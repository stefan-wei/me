    About Me

    Hi, My name is Stefan Wei and I am currently an undergraduate student majoring in Neurobiology, Physiology, and Behavior (NPB) at the University of California, Davis. I am currently a premed student and I hope to enter medical school after I graduate college in 2022. I built this website as a side project during the Covid-19 quarantine after teaching myself HTML and CSS throughout the course of two weeks from Interneting Is Hard. This is mainly a proof of concept project for me as I try to apply everything I learned into this personal website. I'm planning on building a few more websites after I master the basics, with the end goal being able to create a website to help support the elderly and immuno-compromised people in my community here in Davis, CA.
